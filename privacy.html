Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2023

1. Information We Collect

Location Data: HK Transit Tracker requests permission to access your device's precise location (GPS) solely for the purpose of identifying and sorting transit stops closest to you.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your location data is processed entirely locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or log your GPS coordinates on our servers, nor do we share this data with any third parties.

3. Local Storage

The app saves your "Favorite" routes and recent search history locally in your device's browser storage to improve your user experience. This data remains on your device and is not collected by us.

4. Third-Party Services

Transit schedule data is provided by the public DATA.GOV.HK APIs. When the app fetches transit times, standard network requests are made to these APIs, which may log your IP address according to their own privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official app store listing.
